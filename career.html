Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst

🔍

Role Overview

A SOC Analyst monitors, detects, investigates, and responds to cybersecurity threats using SIEM tools, log analysis, and threat intelligence. They work inside a Security Operations Centre to protect organisational assets around the clock.

Why SOC? Ransomware, APTs, and Malaysia's MyDigital initiative are driving demand for SOC analysts across banking, healthcare, and government — requiring 24/7 monitoring coverage.
